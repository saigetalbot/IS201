My Favorite Places to Travel



Hi my name is Saige Talbot and I love to travel!

Here are some places that I have been to with my family

  1. Newport Beach
    • My family loves Newport Beach and we go every year for Thanksgiving break! There are so many things to do. Here are some of the things that we do every time we go.
      • Ride bikes on the boardwalk
      • Beach day
      • Shop at Fashion Island
      • Eat at Mastro's Steakhouse
      • Go to Disneyland

Here is a YouTube tour of Disneyland!

  1. Hawaii
    • My family loves Hawaii as well! We were there for Christmas and for New Years. Here are some of the things that we did while we were there.
      • Go to the beach
      • Beach day
      • Say hi to Santa
      • Watch fireworks on the beach
      • Get tan
  • Lake Powell
    • My family goes to Lake Powell every year over the 4th of July and we always bring lots of fireworks and enjoy the time together with friends and family
